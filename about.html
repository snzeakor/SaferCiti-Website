Our Mission

Built to Save
Nigerian Lives

SaferCiti was created in response to a simple but urgent truth: Nigerians deserve real-time access to the security intelligence that keeps them safe. Every day, millions of people make decisions about where to go, how to travel, and whether it's safe — often without reliable information. We're changing that.

The Problem We Solve

Nigeria faces complex and evolving security challenges — from communal violence in the north to traffic emergencies in Lagos, missing persons crises, and interstate banditry that puts travellers at risk every day.

Before SaferCiti, Nigerians had to rely on fragmented social media posts, unreliable word-of-mouth, or simply hope for the best. There was no single, verified, real-time source of security intelligence built for how Nigerians actually live and move.

SaferCiti changes that. We aggregate, verify, and deliver actionable security intelligence — so that every Nigerian, regardless of location or income level, can make informed decisions about their safety.

Our 5-Layer AI Verification

Not all information is equal. SaferCiti's proprietary 5-layer AI verification system cross-references reports from 39+ Nigerian news sources, community submissions, government alerts, and social media signals to score each incident's credibility before it reaches you.

  • Source credibility scoring across 39+ verified media outlets
  • Cross-referencing multiple independent reports
  • Geolocation validation and LGA mapping
  • Community confirmation or dispute system
  • Temporal relevance weighting (how recent, how active)
